Who we are

We are a group of like-minded outdoor enthusiasts. Binding together by our love our hiking we want to share our passions with you by featuring the best tried-and-tested hiking routes around the UK. We are also passionate about our environments so use our platform to raise awareness of how you can look after your landscapes.

What we do

We collate the best hiking routes, rate them by difficulty, and feature them on our Hikes page in a way that you will easily be able to see the best hikes in your area. We also offer a bespoke hiking service where we will design hiking routes that fit your unique needs. For more inforamtion, head to our 'Tailoured to You' page.
