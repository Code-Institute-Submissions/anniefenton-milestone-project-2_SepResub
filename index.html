Who we are

We are a group of like-minded outdoor enthusiasts. Bound together by our love our hiking and the environment, we want to share our passions with you. We believe the outdoors are for everyone and so aim to make hiking as accessible as possible.

What we do

We collate the best UK hiking routes, rate them by difficulty, and feature them on our interactive map, alongside hiker reviews and other useful information. We've also condensed the country-side code on our 'Sustainability' page so you know how to look after the environment when out hiking. We proudly offer a bespoke hiking service in which we design hiking routes that fit your unique needs. For more information, head to our 'Designed' page.
