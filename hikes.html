Hikes

Featured here are our favourite tried-and-tested routes around the UK. Hover over each map marker to see the name, location, and difficulty of the route. Further information on the hikes can be found below.

Our difficulty ratings

Novice

Suitable for beginner or inexperienced hikers. These routes are mainly flat, with little elevation. Perfect for families.

Intermediate

These routes are a bit more challenging for the more experienced hiker. They will be strenuous but you won't need to be mountain experts to enjoy them.

Expert

Only for the experienced hiker with lots of experience hiking in different environments and conditions. You must be comfortable navigating with a map and compass.
